About

Hi there! My name is Robert. I created Claystone Studies in 2025 as a space to jointly pursue knowledge and collaborate in the sciences, humanities, and philosophy. I have a personal interest in learning and seek to explore and grow. I graduated Cornell University with a B.A. in philosophy. Before starting Claystone Studies, among other things, I offered tutoring services on Varsity Tutors and Wyzant. Here at Claystone Studies, I offer one-on-one tutoring and coaching sessions over Zoom. I also write and am working on a book. You can view my content on YouTube and X.
